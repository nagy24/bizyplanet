FTF COIN

Fawkes has it’s own crypto, which is a BEP-20 token standardizing Binance that can be used for trading purposes, to pay for products online, and can be used to perform DeFi utilities.



FTF STAKING

Join our Community Staking Program and become part of a vibrant and supportive network of like-minded individuals who share a common goal.





RENT-A-TREE

The flagship product of Fawkes is “Rent A Tree” which is created with the objective of motivating everyone to invest and earn a true passive income from an eco-friendly environment while contributing to the green earth.


FAWKES FARM

Step into a virtual countryside and invest in the real world farms while reaping tangible rewards.Join the groundbreaking journey as we harness the potential of the metaverse to create a fertile ground for both virtual growth and real-world prosperity.
